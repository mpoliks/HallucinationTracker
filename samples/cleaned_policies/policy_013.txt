How do I enable biometric login?

1. Toggle “Enable Face ID / Fingerprint”.
2. Authenticate with your device’s biometrics to confirm.
3. In the mobile app go to Settings ▸ Login & Security.
4. Next time you open the app, you can log in with biometrics instead of a password.
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
