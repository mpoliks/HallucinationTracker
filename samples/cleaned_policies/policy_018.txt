How do I view or download tax documents?

1. Log in and select More ▸ Documents ▸ Tax Documents.
2. Choose the tax year; statements are available in PDF.
3. Download or print as needed for your records or tax preparer.
4. Forms (e.g., 1099‑INT) are released by January 31 for the prior year.
If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7 or call 1‑800‑TOGGLE.
