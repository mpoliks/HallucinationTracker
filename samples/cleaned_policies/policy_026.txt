How do I view or download tax documents?

1. Log in and select More ▸ Documents ▸ Tax Documents.
2. Forms (e.g., 1099‑INT) are released by January 31 for the prior year.
3. Download or print as needed for your records or tax preparer.
4. Choose the tax year; statements are available in PDF.
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
