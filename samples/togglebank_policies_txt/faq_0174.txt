What are the different account tiers and how do I qualify?
1. Gold: maintain $10 000 or more across all accounts or direct deposit $2 000+/month.
2. Diamond: invitation‑only, requires $250 000+ in combined balances.
3. Bronze: default tier, no minimum balance.
4. Platinum: maintain $50 000+ or a ToggleBank mortgage.
