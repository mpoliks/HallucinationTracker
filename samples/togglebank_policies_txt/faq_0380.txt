What is the procedure to do I deposit a check using the mobile app?
1. Tap Deposit ▸ Mobile Check Deposit.
2. Keep the physical check for 14 days; funds are usually available the next business day.
3. Endorse the back with “For ToggleBank Mobile Deposit” and today’s date.
4. Snap photos of the front and back ensuring all four corners are visible.
5. Enter the amount and choose the destination account.
