What is ToggleBank’s policy on wire transfer cut‑off times?
1. International wires: 3 PM ET.
2. Domestic wires: 5 PM ET on business days.
3. Requests submitted after cut‑off are processed the next business day.
4. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
