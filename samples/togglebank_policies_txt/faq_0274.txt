What is the process for setting a travel notice?
1. You’ll receive an email confirmation; notices can be edited or canceled anytime.
2. In the mobile app go to Card Controls ▸ Travel Notice.
3. Confirm contact number in case of fraud alerts abroad.
4. Enter destination country, start date, and end date (up to 90 days).
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
