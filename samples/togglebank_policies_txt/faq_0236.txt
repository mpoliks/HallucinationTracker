How do I deposit a check using the mobile app?
1. Endorse the back with “For ToggleBank Mobile Deposit” and today’s date.
2. Keep the physical check for 14 days; funds are usually available the next business day.
3. Enter the amount and choose the destination account.
4. Snap photos of the front and back ensuring all four corners are visible.
5. Tap Deposit ▸ Mobile Check Deposit.
