What is the procedure to do I deposit a check using the mobile app?
1. Enter the amount and choose the destination account.
2. Keep the physical check for 14 days; funds are usually available the next business day.
3. Tap Deposit ▸ Mobile Check Deposit.
4. Endorse the back with “For ToggleBank Mobile Deposit” and today’s date.
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
