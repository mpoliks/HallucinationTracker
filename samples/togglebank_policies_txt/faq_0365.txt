What is the procedure to do I close my account?
1. Download or export any statements you need for your records.
2. Call 1‑800‑TOGGLE or visit a branch to submit the closure request.
3. Ensure your balance is $0 or transfer remaining funds.
4. You’ll receive written confirmation once the account is closed (may take up to 5 business days).
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
