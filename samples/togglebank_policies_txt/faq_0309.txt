What is the procedure to do I enable two‑factor authentication (2FA)?
1. 2FA is required for high‑risk actions like adding new payees or changing contact info.
2. Choose SMS, Email, or Authenticator App.
3. Toggle “Two‑Factor Authentication”.
4. Enter the verification code to confirm activation.
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
