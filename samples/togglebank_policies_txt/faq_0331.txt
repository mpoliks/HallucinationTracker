What is the procedure to do I enable two‑factor authentication (2FA)?
1. Log in and go to Settings ▸ Security.
2. 2FA is required for high‑risk actions like adding new payees or changing contact info.
3. Choose SMS, Email, or Authenticator App.
4. Enter the verification code to confirm activation.
5. Toggle “Two‑Factor Authentication”.
6. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
