Could you explain what should I do if my debit card is lost or stolen?
1. Review recent transactions with the agent; disputed items are refunded provisionally in 10 business days.
2. Open the ToggleBank mobile app and tap Card Controls ▸ Lock Card.
3. Call 1‑800‑TOGGLE within 24 hours to report the loss (available 24/7).
4. A replacement card ships via priority mail within 3–5 calendar days (free for Gold tier and above).
