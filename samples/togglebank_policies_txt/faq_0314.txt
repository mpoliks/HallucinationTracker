What is the procedure to can I dispute an unauthorized transaction?
1. Tap “Report Issue” and choose “Unauthorized”.
2. Navigate to Transactions in the mobile app and select the charge.
3. You’ll receive status updates via push notification and email.
4. Provide any supporting details or receipts; photos can be uploaded.
5. ToggleBank immediately places a temporary credit on your account while we investigate (may take up to 45 days).
