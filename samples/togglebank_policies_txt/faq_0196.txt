What is the procedure to do I request a credit limit increase?
1. You’ll receive approval or further steps instantly in most cases.
2. Navigate to Cards ▸ Credit Card ▸ Manage ▸ Request Limit Increase.
3. A soft credit pull is performed (does not affect credit score).
4. Provide current annual income and monthly housing payment.
