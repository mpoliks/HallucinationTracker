What is the procedure to do I request a credit limit increase?
1. Provide current annual income and monthly housing payment.
2. A soft credit pull is performed (does not affect credit score).
3. You’ll receive approval or further steps instantly in most cases.
4. Navigate to Cards ▸ Credit Card ▸ Manage ▸ Request Limit Increase.
