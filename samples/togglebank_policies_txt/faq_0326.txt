Could you explain what is ToggleBank’s privacy policy regarding customer data?
1. For a full policy see togglebank.com/privacy.
2. Data is shared only with service providers who support banking operations and are bound by contract to safeguard it.
3. Customers may opt out of certain marketing communications under Settings ▸ Privacy.
4. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
