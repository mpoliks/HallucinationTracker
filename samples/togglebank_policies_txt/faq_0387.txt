What is the procedure to can I dispute an unauthorized transaction?
1. Navigate to Transactions in the mobile app and select the charge.
2. Tap “Report Issue” and choose “Unauthorized”.
3. Provide any supporting details or receipts; photos can be uploaded.
4. ToggleBank immediately places a temporary credit on your account while we investigate (may take up to 45 days).
5. You’ll receive status updates via push notification and email.
6. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
