How do I reset my online banking password?
1. From the login screen choose “Forgot Password”.
2. Enter your username and the last 4 digits of your Social Security Number.
3. Select a delivery method to receive a one‑time verification code.
4. Enter the code within 10 minutes and choose a new strong password.
5. Log in with the new password; the change takes effect immediately.
