What is the procedure to do I enable biometric login?
1. Toggle “Enable Face ID / Fingerprint”.
2. In the mobile app go to Settings ▸ Login & Security.
3. Next time you open the app, you can log in with biometrics instead of a password.
4. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
