How do I request a credit limit increase?
1. A soft credit pull is performed (does not affect credit score).
2. Provide current annual income and monthly housing payment.
3. Navigate to Cards ▸ Credit Card ▸ Manage ▸ Request Limit Increase.
4. You’ll receive approval or further steps instantly in most cases.
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
