What is the procedure to do I reset my online banking password?
1. Enter the code within 10 minutes and choose a new strong password.
2. From the login screen choose “Forgot Password”.
3. Enter your username and the last 4 digits of your Social Security Number.
4. Log in with the new password; the change takes effect immediately.
5. Select a delivery method to receive a one‑time verification code.
