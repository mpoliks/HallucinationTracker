How can I add an external account for transfers?
1. Verify the amounts in the app to activate the link.
2. Go to Transfers ▸ Add External Account.
3. You can then schedule one‑time or recurring transfers up to $10 000 per day.
4. Enter the routing and account numbers.
5. ToggleBank sends two micro‑deposits within 1‑2 business days.
