What is the procedure to can I add an external account for transfers?
1. ToggleBank sends two micro‑deposits within 1‑2 business days.
2. You can then schedule one‑time or recurring transfers up to $10 000 per day.
3. Verify the amounts in the app to activate the link.
4. Enter the routing and account numbers.
5. Go to Transfers ▸ Add External Account.
6. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
