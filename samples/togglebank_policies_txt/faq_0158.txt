What is the procedure to do I enable two‑factor authentication (2FA)?
1. Choose SMS, Email, or Authenticator App.
2. 2FA is required for high‑risk actions like adding new payees or changing contact info.
3. Log in and go to Settings ▸ Security.
4. Toggle “Two‑Factor Authentication”.
