How can I dispute an unauthorized transaction?
1. Navigate to Transactions in the mobile app and select the charge.
2. You’ll receive status updates via push notification and email.
3. ToggleBank immediately places a temporary credit on your account while we investigate (may take up to 45 days).
4. Tap “Report Issue” and choose “Unauthorized”.
5. Provide any supporting details or receipts; photos can be uploaded.
