How do I enable two‑factor authentication (2FA)?
1. 2FA is required for high‑risk actions like adding new payees or changing contact info.
2. Log in and go to Settings ▸ Security.
3. Enter the verification code to confirm activation.
4. Choose SMS, Email, or Authenticator App.
5. Toggle “Two‑Factor Authentication”.
