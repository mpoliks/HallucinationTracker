What is the procedure to do I deposit a check using the mobile app?
1. Keep the physical check for 14 days; funds are usually available the next business day.
2. Endorse the back with “For ToggleBank Mobile Deposit” and today’s date.
3. Tap Deposit ▸ Mobile Check Deposit.
4. Snap photos of the front and back ensuring all four corners are visible.
