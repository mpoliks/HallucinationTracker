Could you explain what are the different account tiers and how do I qualify?
1. Bronze: default tier, no minimum balance.
2. Platinum: maintain $50 000+ or a ToggleBank mortgage.
3. Silver: maintain average daily balance of $1 000 or more.
4. Gold: maintain $10 000 or more across all accounts or direct deposit $2 000+/month.
5. Diamond: invitation‑only, requires $250 000+ in combined balances.
6. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
