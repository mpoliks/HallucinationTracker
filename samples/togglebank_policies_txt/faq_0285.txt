How do I deposit a check using the mobile app?
1. Endorse the back with “For ToggleBank Mobile Deposit” and today’s date.
2. Snap photos of the front and back ensuring all four corners are visible.
3. Keep the physical check for 14 days; funds are usually available the next business day.
4. Tap Deposit ▸ Mobile Check Deposit.
5. Enter the amount and choose the destination account.
6. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
