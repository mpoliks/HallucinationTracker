How do I enable two‑factor authentication (2FA)?
1. 2FA is required for high‑risk actions like adding new payees or changing contact info.
2. Enter the verification code to confirm activation.
3. Log in and go to Settings ▸ Security.
4. Toggle “Two‑Factor Authentication”.
5. Choose SMS, Email, or Authenticator App.
