Could you explain what should I do if my debit card is lost or stolen?
1. A replacement card ships via priority mail within 3–5 calendar days (free for Gold tier and above).
2. Call 1‑800‑TOGGLE within 24 hours to report the loss (available 24/7).
3. Open the ToggleBank mobile app and tap Card Controls ▸ Lock Card.
4. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
