How do I set up balance alerts?
1. Go to Settings ▸ Alerts ▸ Balance.
2. Save preferences; alerts are sent in real time when the threshold is crossed.
3. Choose threshold type: fixed dollar amount or percentage of average balance.
4. Select delivery method: push notification, SMS, or email.
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
