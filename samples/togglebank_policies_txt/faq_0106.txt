What should I do if my debit card is lost or stolen?
1. Review recent transactions with the agent; disputed items are refunded provisionally in 10 business days.
2. Open the ToggleBank mobile app and tap Card Controls ▸ Lock Card.
3. A replacement card ships via priority mail within 3–5 calendar days (free for Gold tier and above).
