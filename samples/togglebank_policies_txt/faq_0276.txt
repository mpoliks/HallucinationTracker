What is the procedure to can I add an external account for transfers?
1. ToggleBank sends two micro‑deposits within 1‑2 business days.
2. Verify the amounts in the app to activate the link.
3. Enter the routing and account numbers.
4. Go to Transfers ▸ Add External Account.
5. You can then schedule one‑time or recurring transfers up to $10 000 per day.
6. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
