What is the procedure to do I enable biometric login?
1. Toggle “Enable Face ID / Fingerprint”.
2. Next time you open the app, you can log in with biometrics instead of a password.
3. Authenticate with your device’s biometrics to confirm.
4. In the mobile app go to Settings ▸ Login & Security.
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
