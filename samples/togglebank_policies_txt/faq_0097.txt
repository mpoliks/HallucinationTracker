How do I enable two‑factor authentication (2FA)?
1. Log in and go to Settings ▸ Security.
2. Enter the verification code to confirm activation.
3. Toggle “Two‑Factor Authentication”.
4. Choose SMS, Email, or Authenticator App.
5. 2FA is required for high‑risk actions like adding new payees or changing contact info.
6. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
