How can I dispute an unauthorized transaction?

1. Navigate to Transactions in the mobile app and select the charge.
2. Provide any supporting details or receipts; photos can be uploaded.
3. Tap “Report Issue” and choose “Unauthorized”.
4. You’ll receive status updates via push notification and email.
5. ToggleBank immediately places a temporary credit on your account while we investigate (may take up to 45 days).
6. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
