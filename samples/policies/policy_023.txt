How do I request a credit limit increase?

1. You’ll receive approval or further steps instantly in most cases.
2. Navigate to Cards ▸ Credit Card ▸ Manage ▸ Request Limit Increase.
3. Provide current annual income and monthly housing payment.
4. A soft credit pull is performed (does not affect credit score).
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
5. Factors considered include payment history, credit score, income verification, and account standing.
6. If declined, you may reapply after 60 days or after improving credit factors.
