How do I set up balance alerts?

1. Save preferences; alerts are sent in real time when the threshold is crossed.
2. Go to Settings ▸ Alerts ▸ Balance.
3. Select delivery method: push notification, SMS, or email.
4. Choose threshold type: fixed dollar amount or percentage of average balance.
5. Multiple alerts can be set for different thresholds and accounts.
6. Alerts help prevent overdrafts and monitor unusual account activity.
