What is the process for setting a travel notice?

1. Enter destination country, start date, and end date (up to 90 days).
2. Confirm contact number in case of fraud alerts abroad.
3. In the mobile app go to Card Controls ▸ Travel Notice.
4. You’ll receive an email confirmation; notices can be edited or canceled anytime.
5. If you need further assistance, contact ToggleSupport via chat 24/7.
